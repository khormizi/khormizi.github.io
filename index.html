About Me

I'm a Staff Applied Scientist at Cruise, where I lead cross-functional initiatives in autonomous vehicle planning and prediction. My work spans end-to-end driving models, trajectory optimization, behavior prediction, and production ML systems that operate on real roads.

Before Cruise, I was a Senior AI/ML Research Scientist at LG Electronics, working on robotics path planning, V2X misbehavior detection, and computer vision optimization.

I hold a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and an M.S. in Mathematics from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. My doctoral research focused on quickest statistical inference over networked data.
